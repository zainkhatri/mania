# mania: My Journaling Style, Now in an App

**mania** is an app I’m building to bring my personal journaling style to life. I love journaling with colorful titles, photo collages of my friends and me, neatly formatted dates, and a clean layout. This app is all about making that style easy to create and organize digitally.

## Features

- **Custom Journal Entries**  
  Create journal entries with text and photos. Whether it’s a quick thought or a detailed memory, everything is formatted to match my style.

- **Photo Collages**  
  Add photo collages to your entries with tools for positioning, resizing, and overlaying images. It's a fun way to include memories visually.

- **Organized Layouts**  
  View your entries in either a grid or list format, making it easy to browse through your journal.

- **Automatic Date Formatting**  
  The app takes care of formatting dates so you don’t have to, keeping your entries consistent and organized.

- **Clean and Minimal Design**  
  A black-and-white theme keeps everything simple and focused on your content.

- **Local Storage**  
  Everything is stored on your device, so your journal stays private and always accessible.
